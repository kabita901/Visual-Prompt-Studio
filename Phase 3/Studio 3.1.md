Prompt 1
Historical Theme: Art as divine power
Historical Period: Ancient Egypt
Artwork Inspiration: Khafre with the Falcon God Horus

Visual prompt: Create a monumental Ancient Egyptian stone sculpture carved from dark, polished diorite. Depict a seated pharaoh in a rigid, frontal pose with a symmetrical, block like form emphasizing stability and permanence. His idealized, muscular body should be simplified into smooth geometric shapes with strong vertical and horizontal lines. Behind his head, include a falcon with outstretched wings wrapping protectively around him, its feathers stylized into repeating rhythmic patterns. Use hierarchical scale so the pharaoh appears dominant over all symbols. The surface should be highly polished to subtly reflect light, reinforcing divine perfection. The composition must feel closed, compact, and timeless, with no deep space, expressing eternal kingship and divine authority.


Prompt 2
Historical Theme: Humanism and the move toward science
Historical Period: Early Renaissance
Artwork Inspiration: Giotto Lamentation

Visual prompt: Create an Early Renaissance fresco inspired by Giotto’s Lamentation. Depict mourners gathered around a fallen figure, arranged using diagonal lines that guide the viewer’s eye toward the emotional center. Place the figures in a shallow but believable space with early linear perspective. Include a rocky landscape sloping inward to create depth. Figures should have naturalistic proportions, weight, and drapery that follows the contours of the body, emphasizing volume. Show expressive grief through furrowed brows, downcast eyes, and varied gestures. Use cool, muted colors soft blues, earthy browns, pale flesh tones to unify the somber mood. The sky should be a flat blue field, bridging medieval stylization with emerging realism and human emotion.
