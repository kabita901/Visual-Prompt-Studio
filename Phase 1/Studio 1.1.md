Tittle: Beneath the cracks
Prompt:Painted on the side of an abandoned community center in an urban neighborhood affected by housing inequality.
Vocabulary Breakdown:
Medium: Mural painting using mixed acrylics and spray paint
Subject Mattern: The mural depicts a large pair of hands emerging from cracked pavement, lifting small houses upward toward a bright sky. Around the edges, faint silhouettes of families stand watching, emphasizing the human impact of the crisis.
Place Where the Artwork Can Be Found: Painted on the side of an abandoned community center in an urban neighborhood affected by housing inequality.
Social Role: This mural acts as a form of protest by bringing attention to the ongoing issue of affordable housing shortages. It inspires discussion among community members and highlights the neglect of underserved neighborhoods.
